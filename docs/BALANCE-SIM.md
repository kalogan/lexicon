## Methodology & caveats

- **Word source:** `an-array-of-english-words` (274804 words, ≥3 letters) — the game's own dictionary.
- **Bot word choice:** greedy — the single highest-scoring legal word each play (real engine `scoreWord`, boss `allow` rule, sealed cells, gold tile all honoured). A human deck-builder plays a sequence (Encore/Serpent chains, Alphabet coverage) the greedy bot doesn't optimise for, so combo relics are UNDER-valued here.
- **Shop policy:** Buy most expensive affordable relic. Deck edits (add/remove tile) are NOT exercised — the bot keeps its opening deck, so deck-craft's contribution is out of scope.
- **Discards:** the bot re-deals a board when its best word is far below what's needed and a discard remains.
- **Lift is correlational, not causal:** relics co-occur within a run, so a strong engine relic lifts its shelf-mates too. Read lift alongside *owned* count. A hoarding policy owns most of the pool each run, which compresses lift toward zero for everyone — compare policies.
- **Determinism:** every run is seeded; re-running with the same seed base reproduces these numbers. Runtime: 329.7s for 150 runs.
